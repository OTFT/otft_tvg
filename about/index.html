OTFT: The Videogame

Who is OTFT?

OTFT (The person) is an indie game developer with a passion for his game franchise, which he is developing with his best friend, JJ Productions.

The entire conception of OTFT had began in April 2018, the creation of the name, and then in November 2018, the first drawing of OTFT. Originally, it started as a game mod idea, using 1 original character and 2 copyrighted characters, but soon the following year, it evolved into its own original idea.

OTFT's design has gone through a plethora of changes throughout the years, and below are a couple highlights.


2018: The first drawing

This is the first ever drawing of OTFT, created in 2018. OTFT has weirdly sharp eyes with a backwards cap on, and the logo on his shirt is a smirking face. He's also wearing a ripped jean jacket, and he has spikey shoes on.


2019

OTFT's design has been slightly altered. His shirt and cap now have an "O" on it, very reminicent of the current "O" used today, and his jacket is now a leather jacket. The original team is also shown beside him.


2021

OTFT's backwards cap has been entirely ditched, instead now he has a downwards hairdo. His shirt is a stylized "O", which reoccurs even to today, with the special fontface of this website.


2024

OTFT's final design.
