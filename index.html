About

OTFT Studios is a company by OTFTYT Created in 2018 with the first game in development being Team OTFT. For more information about the game, visit the game's page.

Other Major Projects

OTFT has been working on other projects as well, like OGDPS and Rolling Sky World. OGDPS is a remake of GD to be connected to a different server, add 2 levels, and look appealing. It has a ton of uploaded levels that are fun and worth checking out. Rolling Sky World is a fangame of Rolling Sky that puts Skyball in an open-world area. It has been since cancelled temporarly and is not being worked on for the forseeable future.
